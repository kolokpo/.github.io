OPEN CALL.

The basis of our method of creative work is to explore. We pursue improvisation, working on samples, composing conceptual and illustrative music. The instrumentation we use is both analogue (piano, synthesizers, chordophones, drums, vocals) and digital (DAW software, electronic drums, effects and synthesizers). We create experimental music and audiovisual etudes. We cooperate with movie directors and compose illustrative music for film projects and commercials.

The core of the band are Wojciech Unterschuetz and Piotr Bratoszewski. Based in Gdańsk, Poland.
